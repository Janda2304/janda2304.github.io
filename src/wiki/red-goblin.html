Red Goblin

The Red Goblin is the first enemy featured in Fortress Guardian. Renowned for its simplicity and early appearance, the Red Goblin serves as the introductory enemy, making its debut in wave 1 of the training grounds.


Characteristics
The Red Goblin boasts straightforward attributes, embodying the essence of simplicity within the game's enemy hierarchy. It possesses a modest health pool of 40 hit points and inflicts 3 points of damage per strike. With a speed rating of 2.3, it maneuvers through the game world with relative agility, posing a moderate challenge to novice players.


Gameplay
As the easiest enemy encountered within the game, the Red Goblin serves as an ideal starting point for players to familiarize themselves with combat mechanics and tactics. Its appearance in wave 1 of the training grounds marks the initiation of the player's journey, offering a gentle introduction to the challenges that lie ahead. With an attack speed averaging approximately 0.8 strikes per second, players have ample opportunity to hone their defensive skills and devise strategies to overcome subsequent, more formidable foes.


Development History
Originally introduced as the "Goblin" during the game's development stages, this enemy underwent a significant evolution in dev0.15, emerging as the enhanced and rebranded "Red Goblin." This transformation not only included a name change but also involved a substantial change of its statistics, further solidifying its role as the introductory enemy to Romardia.


The Red Goblin's legacy as the starting point for players embarking on their guardian's journey sets it as an iconic figure within the world of Romardia.
