The future of Romardia

Greetings guardians of Romardia,
I'm excited to share the exciting feature we are planning for Fortress Guardian. With your support, we're ready to embark on a journey to elevate your gaming experience to new heights.


New Defenses:
As the hordes of enemies grow ever more relentless, we're going to improve our defenses with an array of new towers. From the rugged Stone Barricades, providing sturdy fortifications, to the enigmatic Power Totems, harnessing arcane energies and boosting other towers, these new additions will offer fresh strategies to your gameplay.


New Maps:
The world of Romardia pulsates with elemental energies, and we're going to harness these forces to introduce dynamic environments through our cutting-edge in-house voxel terrain engine. From raging infernos to icy blizzards, these new environments will challenge your adaptability and inspire you to use new tactics to overcome the ever-changing landscape.


Multiplayer (Far, far away):
I'm excited to announce the addition of a cooperative multiplayer mode.
In the future you'll be able to join forces with your friends or fellow adventurers from across the realm and face dangers of Romardia together.


Campaign Improvements:
Prepare to delve deeper into the lore of Romardia.
Embark on an epic journey, where every victory brings you closer to unlocking the secrets of our world.
With new maps, new quest system and challenges that will test your skills, the campaign mode will be more engaging and rewarding than ever before.


Improved Visuals:
Romardia is more than just a battleground, it's a world of beauty waiting to be explored.
With enhanced visuals, we're gonna bring every corner of our realm to life in detail. You'll be able to immerse yourself in the beauty of Romardia like never before.


Community Feedback:
Remember, your feedback is the guiding light that illuminates our path forward.
I'm always listening to your feedback to ensure that Fortress Guardian will continue to evolve in the best way possible.


Together, we'll continue to defend the realm of Romardia and forge a legacy that will be remembered for ages. Thank you for your dedication to Fortress Guardian, and may your towers and fortresses stand strong against the invading hordes.

Until our next adventure,

Ondrej Janousek
Lead Developer of Fortress Guardian
