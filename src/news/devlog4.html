Enhanced save system and endless mode

Greetings, Guardians!

We're back with an exciting update on Fortress Guardian, introducing a revamped save system and an exciting new game mode.
Let's dive into the details of what's in store for you in the latest update!

Infinite save file support

We heard your feedback loud and clear,
and we're thrilled to announce that Fortress Guardian now supports infinite save files!
Whether you're a solo strategist or sharing your gaming experience with friends and family, there's no limit to the number of save files you can create.

Profiles for Map Progression

Say goodbye to linear progression!
With our new profile system, each player can now maintain separate save data for each map.
Whether you're honing your skills in the Training Grounds or exploring the dense forest of Magical Forest, your progress on each map is now saved independently.
This allows for greater flexibility and replayability, empowering players to revisit their favorite maps or conquer new challenges at their own pace.

new save file visualised into a diagram

Endless Mode

Prepare yourselves for the ultimate test of endurance with the introduction of Endless Mode!
In this game mode, waves of enemies will assault your fortress endlessly.
But fear not, your efforts will not be worthless.
Endless Mode features its own dedicated save, allowing you to track your progress and compete for even better results.
How long can you hold out against the endless horde?

Code-Wise Improvements and Optimizations

Behind the scenes, I've been working hard implementing a lot of code improvements and optimizations.
These enhancements ensure that any other changes to the save system will be implemented much faster and will not break your old saves.
I'm committed to delivering the best gaming experience possible that allows you to focus on what truly matters: defending your fortress.

Thank you for your ongoing support and feedback. Together, we'll fortify our defenses and emerge victorious against the forces of darkness in Romardia!

See you on the battlefield, Guardians!

Ondrej Janousek

Lead Developer of Fortress Guardian
