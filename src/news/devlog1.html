Romardia's Landscapes reimagined

Map Expansion and Optimization:
In latest update, I've scaled Romardia's maps approximately four times to accommodate the introduction of a new fortress building. This expansion provides players with larger space to strategize and fortify their defenses. Additionally, I've optimized grass layers, trees, and overall design to enhance the visual experience and immerse players further into the world of Romardia.


Map size comparison: More Enemy Paths:
Sometimes enemies now have multiple paths to follow, increasing the challenge for players. The addition of more paths introduces more challenge to the world Romardia. Each map also features new, unique origins where enemies come from, such as dense forests, mysterious caves, or sand dunes.
