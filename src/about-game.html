Fortress Guardian

Welcome Guardian

Fortress Guardian is first-person tower defense game, where you defend your fortress against continuous waves of enemies.

Story:
Embark on an epic journey in the enchanting realm of Romardia, set within the galaxy of Ruhitia. Recently, Romardia's largest towns and fortresses have fallen to relentless invasions by unknown forces. Once an elderly sage known as Eldin the Wise, a revered guardian of the ancient fortresses of Romardia, calls upon you. Eldin posesses invaluable wisdom and knowledge of fortress guardianship. He starts to teach you as soon as possible. However, with each passing lesson, Eldin's vitality wanes, leaving you to ponder the weighty responsibility that awaits. Are you prepared to descend to the realm of Romardia safeguarding it against impending doom? Will you rise to the challenge and destroy the forces threatening to shatter the sacred crystal of Ruhitia, thereby preserving the very fabric of Romardia?

Features:
Fully procedural wave generation system
Multiple maps
Multiple enemies and buildings
Complex saving system
4 difficulties
Two camera perspectives
